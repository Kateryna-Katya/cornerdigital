Travel Crush: Match 3

About the Game

Travel meets puzzle gameplay

Every level you beat in Travel Crush feels like you're actually taking another step on your trip. As players complete puzzles, they unlock new destinations and visually move forward across the map. It's this cool mix where you can chill out with the puzzles but still feel like you're going somewhere.

How to Play

  • Easy to start, fun to master

  • Match three or more identical pieces

  • Complete level objectives

  • Use boosters for complex situations

  • Progress through travel locations

  • Unlock new areas as you advance

Core Features

  • Built for engagement and discovery

  • Match-3 gameplay with evolving levels

  • Travel-based progression system

  • Unlockable destinations

  • Colorful and dynamic visuals

  • Short, replayable sessions
